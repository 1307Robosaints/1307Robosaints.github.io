Why should you sponsor us?

Sponsoring the Robosaints would help us trememdously in helping pay off the costs associated with competitions. It would help us pay off entrance and a multitude of other fees for competition. Furthermore, it would assist us in purchasing new, more efficient and effective tools, and new parts to help them perform at their best in every situation. Beyond that, think of your donation as an investement in the new generation. Without your help, we cannot learn the skills needed in life later down the line. With your donation, the robotics team can continue to flourish and teach kids that may very well be the future of engineering.

St. Thomas Aquinas High School

STA is a Catholic high school based in Dover, NH. With the "Education that makes a difference", they teach students all around New Hampshire, Massachusetts, and Maine.
