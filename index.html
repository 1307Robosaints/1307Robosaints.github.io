Welcome to the Home of the Robosaints!

Our Team:

Based in at St. Thomas Aquinas High School in Dover, New Hampshire, we are the Robosaints, Team 1307. We are a team run by students, with mentors with many different skillsets helping us. We compete in the FIRST Robotics Competition, founded by Dean Kamen, the inventor of the segway. The FIRST Robotics Competition takes place worldwide, and winners are international. To learn more about FRC and competitions, click here.

Through competitions and build season, we prototype, build, and test robots. We gain the basic skills used in everyday work. We learn how to use power tools, basic programming skills used to run the robot, electrical basics to wire the robot, and most importantly, the power of teamwork and gracious professionalism.

We welcome and hope you have a great experience on our website. To get started, access the menu at the top, or if you're on a mobile device, press the menu icon in the upper right corner. If there is anything you have questions about, feel free to contact us via the email provided. Thank you!
