About Us

FRC team 1307, known as "the Robosaints", is a group of students in grades 9-12. We all have varying skill levels, and all have their own special skills. Some of us are great at programming, others at designing robots, others at organizing the pit, and more. We love robotics, and are proud to call the Notre Damne Makerspace our home!

Team Leaders

Jacob Kosko

Robotics Head of Operations/President

A senior at Saint Thomas, and longtime member of our team, Jacob has attained a (high school) level of mastery in electrical engineering, machining, and mechanical design. He runs the team like a professional, and is truly a team player.

Andrew Krupski

Vice President, Head of Safety

A senior at STA, Krupski is one of the team's vice presidents. He also holds the title of "Captain Safety", formally known as the head of safety on our team. He has been a part of the team since his first year at STA. Outside of Robotics, he has a big passion for graphic design, and always brightens the mood of the room he's in!

Andrew Tsouprakakis - "Soupy"

Lead Programmer, Head of Technology, Soon-to-be President

A junior at STA, Soupy is the lead programmer for the team, and as such is in charge of training and mentoring new programmers, as well as managing the robot's codebase. He is a personable member of the team, and is on track to become the president of the Robosaints during his senior year.

Elliot Duprat

Vice President

Also a senior at STA, Elliot is the other vice president of the team. He is an integral part of our group of drivers, and our team wouldn't be the same without him.

Daniel Lowry

Main Driver, Builder/Mechanic

Currently in his second year at STA, Danny enjoys building and designing the bot, and he is our best driver. He is a great team member, and has a sufficient sense of humor.
