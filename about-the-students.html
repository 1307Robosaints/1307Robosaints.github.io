About Us

The STA Robotics Team 1307 is a group of students in grades 9-12. We all have varying skill levels, and all have their own special skills. Some of us are great at programming, others at designing robots, others at organizing the pit, and more. We love robotics, and probably spend more time in the robotics than we do at home!
